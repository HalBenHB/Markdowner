### OBJECTIVE AND LEARNING OUTCOMES
**Educational objective**
To familiarize students with corporate finance theory and techniques and enable them to apply those in real world situations.

**Course Description**
This course provides a thorough overview of corporate finance through the study of decision making within corporations. The course analyzes important issues in corporate finance from the perspective of financial managers who are responsible for making significant investment and financing decisions. The first part of the course provides a discussion on capital market efficiency and an overview of the financing decisions of corporations. The second part of the course includes a detailed analysis of financial leverage, capital structure and dividend policy as they relate to the value-maximization objective of the corporation.

**Learning outcomes**
- **LO 1:** Describe in detail the financial leverage, capital structure and dividend policy of a firm.
- **LO 2:** Show the relationship between financial manager’s decisions and firm value.
- **LO 3:** Use online resources to find the data to analyze a firm’s long-term financial policy.
- **LO 4:** Apply basic corporate finance principals to international corporations.
- **LO 5:** Define different types of mergers and acquisitions and gains from M&A activities.
